Arcane: League of Legends

Plot

Arcane tells the story of two sisters, Vi and Jinx, who are torn apart by conflict in the divided cities of Piltover and Zaun. As Piltover thrives with the invention of Hextech, Zaun’s citizens turn to the dangerous drug Shimmer for power. While Vi fights for justice, Jinx descends into chaos, setting the stage for a war fueled by ambition, technology, and betrayal.
